About Exciting Expeditions

History

Exciting Expeditions was founded to bring quality rafting experiences to families, schools, and adventurers. Starting with a few rafts, we now run dozens of safe and exciting trips each month.

Our guides are trained and certified in river safety and first aid. We continuously review safety procedures and keep our equipment well-maintained to ensure safe, memorable trips.
